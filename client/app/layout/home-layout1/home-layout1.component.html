Thorina Apartments

The apartments Blue Flower and Red Sea contains: the living room which is connected to a big terrace(with beautiful sea view) and with kitchen in which you have coffee machine, microwave oven, dishwasher machine, toaster, fridge and freezer. The other appliances in the apartment are iron, ironing board, vacuum cleaner and hair dryer, air condition, the TV-satellite. There are bedroom with double bed(and linen), children room with 2 single beds, bathroom with shower and towers. In front of apartment is a parking place and the grill. When you decide to come, you just need to bring only a smile.
